Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect and store the following information:

  • Account information (email address and password hash)
  • Expense entries you create (amount, category, date, description)
  • Usage data to improve our service

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the expense tracking service
  • To display your personal financial summaries and insights
  • To improve user experience and app functionality
  • To communicate important service updates

Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your information is stored in encrypted databases with access controls. Passwords are hashed using secure algorithms before storage. We regularly review our security practices to ensure your data remains protected.

Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may use trusted service providers for hosting and analytics, but they are bound by confidentiality agreements and cannot use your data for their own purposes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us. We are committed to addressing your concerns and ensuring your privacy is respected.
